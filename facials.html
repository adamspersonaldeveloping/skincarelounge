Facial Treatments

I am a Board Certified Aesthetician

I offer several non-invasive, minimal downtime treatments for those who desire firmer, younger looking skin without risky surgery or painful injections. These treatments repair dull, lifeless skin to reveal newer, more radiant skin. Corrective treatments are different from relaxing facials as they target specific skin concerns, such as minimizing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, enlarged pores, acne and acne scars, texture, age spots, and hyperpigmentation.

Treatments

Microdemabrasion

Our top facial treatment with no down time. Improves acne scars. Helps to treat premature aging and sun damaged skin. It helps to buff away fine lines and gradually diminishes hyper pigmentation. It helps to soften,smooth and rejuvenate. Finishing with a nourishing mask. Your makeup will go on smooth and silky.

Microdermabrasion treatment with massage $100

Microdermabrasion targeted treatment (30 min) $59

Microdermabrasion add-on treatment (15 min) $25

Dermaplane

Dermaplaning is a minimally invasive procedure that shaves away the top layers of your skin. It can help reduce the appearance of acne scars or pitted skin. Some people get dermaplaning to remove unwanted hair.

Dermaplane with mask and hydrating treatment $85

Dermaplane basic (40 min) $60

Dermaplane add-on treatment (15 min) $40

Hydrafacial

A hydrafacial is one of the most powerful, non-invasive skin resurfacing treatments available today. It combines cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, hydration and antioxidant protection that removes dead skin cells and impurities, while simultaneously delivering moisturizing serums into the skin.
